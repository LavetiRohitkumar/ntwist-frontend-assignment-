FAQ

What is NTWIST?

NTWIST is a software company focused on industrial optimization.

What are we ?

We are a team of diverse process and data engineers who are passionate about boosting industrial sustainability, efficiency and excellence by using cutting edge AI/ML technologies

What do we do ?

NTWIST helps Oil & Gas customers to analyze historical data, identify opportunities for process control improvements, and reduce variability in any part of the process, thus providing enhanced reliability and safety.

What we offer ?

NTWIST offers a new product that unlocks up to $250/oz in previously inaccessible value in open pit gold mines by connecting siloed data sources like block models, fleet management systems, stockpile surveys, and plant instruments.
